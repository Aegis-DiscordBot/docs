## Overview

The `ban` command permanently removes a member from your server and prevents them from rejoining unless unbanned.

## Usage
$ban @user [reason]

Or with a slash command:

/ban user: @user reason: [reason]

## Options

| Option | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `user` | ✅ | The member to ban |
| `reason` | ❌ | The reason for the ban |
| `delete_messages` | ❌ | How many days of messages to delete (0-7) |

## Examples
$ban @alexxxzdevelops Spamming in general

$ban @alexxxzdevelops

/ban user: @alexxxzdevelops reason:Spamming in general

## Behavior

- The banned member will receive a DM notification with the reason (if they share a server with Aegis)
- The action will be logged in your mod log channel if configured
- The ban is recorded in Aegis's database and visible in the dashboard

<Warning>
  You cannot ban members with a higher or equal role than yours.
</Warning>
