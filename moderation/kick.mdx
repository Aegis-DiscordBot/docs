## Overview

The `kick` command removes a member from your server. Unlike a ban, kicked members can rejoin using a valid invite link.

## Usage
$kick @user [reason]

Or with a slash command:

/kick user: @user reason: [reason]

## Options

| Option | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `user` | ✅ | The member to kick |
| `reason` | ❌ | The reason for the kick |

## Examples
$kick @alexxxzdevelops Being disruptive in voice chat

$kick @alexxxzdevelops

/kick user:@alexxxzdevelops reason:Being disruptive in voice chat

## Behavior

- The kicked member will receive a DM notification with the reason (if they share a server with Aegis)
- The action will be logged in your mod log channel if configured
- The kick is recorded in Aegis's database and visible in the dashboard
- The kicked member **can** rejoin the server with a valid invite

<Warning>
  You cannot kick members with a higher or equal role than yours.
</Warning>

<Info>
  If you want to permanently remove a member, use the [Ban](/moderation/ban) command instead.
</Info>
