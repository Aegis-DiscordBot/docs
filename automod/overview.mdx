## What is Automod?

Aegis's Automod system automatically monitors and filters messages in your server based on rules you configure. It works silently in the background, keeping your server clean without requiring manual intervention from moderators.

<Info>
  Automod actions are separate from manual moderation actions and are logged independently in your automod log channel.
</Info>

## How It Works

When a message violates an Automod rule, Aegis will:

1. **Delete** the offending message instantly
2. **Warn** the member (optional, configurable)
3. **Log** the incident to your automod log channel
4. **DM** the member explaining why their message was removed (optional)

## Ignored Roles & Channels

You can configure roles and channels that are completely ignored by Automod.
$automod ignore role @Moderator

$automod ignore channel #mod-chat

$automod unignore role @Moderator

$automod unignore channel #mod-chat

<Info>
  Members with the **Administrator** permission are always ignored by Automod.
</Info>

## Quick Setup
$automod setup

This will walk you through enabling Automod and configuring the basic rules for your server.
