## Step 1 — Invite Aegis

Click the button below to invite Aegis to your Discord server.

<Card
  title="Invite Aegis"
  icon="discord"
  href="https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1495395864833949767"
  horizontal
>
  Add Aegis to your server with the recommended permissions.
</Card>

<Warning>
  Make sure Aegis has the following permissions: **Administrator** (recommended) or at minimum **Manage Roles**, **Manage Channels**, **Ban Members**, **Kick Members**, **Moderate Members**, and **View Audit Log**.
</Warning>

## Step 2 — Set Up Aegis

Once Aegis is in your server, run the setup command:
$setup

This will walk you through configuring:

- A mod log channel
- An automod channel
- A join/leave log channel
- Basic automod rules

## Step 3 — Configure the Dashboard

For a more detailed setup, head to the Aegis dashboard and log in with Discord.
