## What is the Music System?

Aegis comes with a built-in music system that lets you play high quality audio directly in your Discord voice channels. Supports YouTube, Spotify, and more — all with a full queue management system.

  <Card title="Volume Control" icon="volume-high">

## Supported Sources

| Source | Supported |
|---|---|
| YouTube | ✅ |
| Spotify | ✅ |
| SoundCloud | ✅ |
| Direct URL | ✅ |
| Apple Music | ❌ |

<Info>
  Spotify links are resolved via YouTube — Aegis finds the best matching track automatically.
</Info>

## Quick Start

Join a voice channel and run:

```bash
$play <song name or URL>
```

Aegis will join your voice channel and start playing immediately.
