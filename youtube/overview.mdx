## What are YouTube Notifications?

Aegis's YouTube notification system automatically alerts your server when a YouTuber you follow posts a new video, short, or goes live. Perfect for content creator communities!

## How It Works

Aegis uses the YouTube Data API v3 to poll for new content from your configured YouTubers. When new content is detected, Aegis sends a notification to your configured channel.

<Info>
  Notifications are checked every 5 minutes. There may be a slight delay between a video being posted and the notification being sent.
</Info>

## Notification Types

| Type | Trigger |
|---|---|
| Video | A new video is uploaded |
| Short | A new short is uploaded |
| Livestream | A livestream starts |
| Livestream End | A livestream ends |

## Quick Setup

```bash
$youtube add <channel URL or ID>
```

Aegis will ask you to configure the notification channel, message, and role ping.
